Izon Beli - Learn Ijaw

Learn the Izon language on the go in the smartest and easiest way possible. This app comes as a much needed contributory effort in the revitalization and protection of the Izon language from extinction.

Developed for the Wakirike Development Coalition, U.S.A, this app was built to teach people how to speak the wakirike language right on their mobile phone. The app features over 25 topics and hundreds of common conversational words and sentences featuring text, sound and graphics. Get it here
