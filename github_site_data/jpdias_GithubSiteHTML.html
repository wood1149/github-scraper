About Me

Engineer. Developer. Photography enthusiast.

Currently working as a researcher on the thin line between hardware and software. Graduated from the Integrated Masters in Informatics and Computing Engineering by Faculty of Engineering, University of Porto (FEUP). Passionate about technology in general, with a special focus on security and privacy, web development & Internet of Things. Furthermore, an amateur photographer, football player, book lover and science addict.
